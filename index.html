// DAY 02 · APRIL 06, 2026

Tech Industry
Company Visits

Our first day of industry immersion — exploring how local and global tech companies operate in the Philippine context.

MORNING

HYTEC POWER INC.

HYTEC Power Inc. is a Filipino company specializing in industrial automation, engineering solutions, educational training systems, and industrial technologies. Established in 1994, the company bridges the gap between education and industry by providing modern engineering and technical training equipment.

Industrial Automation Engineering Solutions Technical Training
  • HYTEC Power Inc. specializes in industrial automation, engineering solutions, educational training systems, and industrial technologies.
  • Established in 1994, the company supports schools and industries by bridging education and real-world technical applications.
  • Main services include industrial automation solutions, engineering training systems, pneumatics and hydraulics, and technical education support.
  • HYTEC is known for providing modern technology solutions and workforce training for educational institutions and industries.
AFTERNOON

OPEN TEXT

A global leader in Enterprise Information Management (EIM) software. Their Manila office handles large-scale software development and tech support for global clients.

Enterprise Software Cloud Solutions Global IT
  • Filipino software developers are competing globally with world-class technical skill.
  • Enterprise solutions require not just coding skills but also strong communication and project management.
  • The IT sector is one of the fastest-growing industries offering high-salary careers in the Philippines.
PHOTOS · DAY 2
HYTEC Power
Energy Demo
OpenText Office
OpenText Office

Seeing the offices of HYTEC Power and OpenText was truly an eye-opener. These are not just ordinary workplaces — they are ecosystems of innovation and continuous learning. What made the experience even more memorable was having the opportunity to operate heavy equipment simulations at HYTEC Power. It was really fun because it felt like playing computer games, but at the same time, it was amazing to realize how advanced technology has become. Through innovation, it is now possible to perform tasks and training through simulations without being physically present on-site. Meanwhile, my visit to OpenText inspired me because of the many career paths and opportunities they offer, giving me a clearer vision of the possibilities in the IT industry.

// DAY 03 · APRIL 07, 2026

Creative Studio
& BPO Giants

MORNING

TOP PEG ANIMATION & CREATIVE STUDIO, INC.

One of the Philippines' premier animation studios, producing world-class 2D and 3D animation for international and local clients.

2D Animation 3D Rendering Creative Arts
  • Filipino animators are producing content for global streaming platforms and movie studios.
  • Animation is a technical craft requiring mastery of both artistic skills and software tools.
  • The creative industry is a viable and growing career path in the Philippines.
AFTERNOON

TELEPERFORMANCE PHILIPPINES INC.

The world's largest customer experience management company. The Philippines is one of their biggest hubs, employing tens of thousands of Filipinos.

BPO Customer Experience Global Operations
  • The BPO industry contributes significantly to the Philippine economy, with billions in annual revenue.
  • Strong communication skills and empathy are just as important as technical knowledge in this field.
  • Teleperformance uses AI tools to augment human agents — the future of CX is hybrid.

Top Peg showed us that art and technology work together. Every animation takes both creativity and technical skills to create. On the other hand, Teleperformance reminded us that even with advanced technology, human skills like understanding, patience, and good communication are still very important and cannot be replaced.

// DAY 04 · APRIL 08, 2026

Government Tech
& BGC Night

MORNING

MMDA COMMAND CENTER

The Metropolitan Manila Development Authority's nerve center — managing traffic, disasters, and public safety for 17 cities using real-time data systems.

Smart City IoT
AFTERNOON

MICROSOURCING PHILIPPINES

Australia's largest offshore staffing provider in the Philippines. They connect Australian businesses with Filipino professionals in a managed office environment.

Outsourcing Remote Work
EVENING

BONIFACIO GLOBAL CITY

A walk through BGC's modern urban landscape — a planned, walkable city district that is home to dozens of multinational companies, restaurants, and galleries.

Urban Planning Nightlife
The MMDA Command Center felt like seeing how a city works behind the scenes. Many screens were used to watch major roads, intersections, and flood-prone areas in real time. We saw how technology such as cameras, sensors, and data helps manage traffic and city problems. It showed us that computer science and engineering can also be used to help the public and improve how cities are managed.
Microsourcing showed us the global dimension of the Philippine workforce. Filipino professionals are working for Australian, American, and European companies without leaving the country. It emphasized that developing English proficiency, digital literacy, and specialized skills can open doors to international jobs right here at home. The office environment was incredibly professional and collaborative.

These different experiences showed why learning through real experiences is very meaningful and effective.

// REFLECTIONS & LEARNINGS

What We Took
Home With Us

More than just pictures and souvenirs, this tour gave us lessons and experiences that we will carry as students and future professionals.

Technology is Everywhere

Visiting companies like MMDA, HYTEC, OpenText, MicroSourcing, and Teleperformance showed us how important technology is in different industries. We realized that IT skills are useful almost everywhere.

Filipino Excellence is Real

We met many talented Filipinos working in different fields. Their stories inspired us to work harder and believe that Filipinos can compete globally.

History Grounds Us

Visiting Intramuros and Fort Santiago reminded us about the sacrifices of our heroes. It made us appreciate our history and freedom even more.

Soft Skills Matter

One thing we learned from the companies we visited is that communication, teamwork, and adaptability are just as important as technical skills.

Nature Helps Us Recharge

Our trips to Tagaytay and Baguio gave us time to relax and enjoy nature. It reminded us that taking breaks is important for our mental health.

Have a Purpose

The people we met were passionate about their work. We learned that having a goal and purpose can motivate us to do better in life and in our future careers.

// FINAL REFLECTION
"We came home with more than just memories."

This educational tour helped us learn outside the classroom. We gained new experiences, discovered different industries, visited historical places, and created unforgettable memories together. It was not just a trip — it was a meaningful journey that inspired us for our future.
